The Buzz Cut

This term can refer to almost any cut a barber achieves with a pair of clippers that renders the side of your head nearly bare but guys like Pharrell Williams get nearly bald all over. Variations of this theme include the crew cut, the high-and-tight, and the undercut, but more on those in a moment.
